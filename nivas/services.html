Pricing

Three packages, one standard of work.

Prices are per square foot of finished modular area. The package changes the materials and hardware — never the care taken in fitting them.

How to read these numbers. A typical 8×10 ft L-shaped kitchen works out to roughly 90–110 sq ft of modular area, so a Signature kitchen usually lands between ₹1.75 lakh and ₹2.15 lakh. Counters, chimneys, hobs and sinks are quoted separately so you can choose your own brands. After a site visit you get a written, line-by-line quotation — that number is the one we hold to.

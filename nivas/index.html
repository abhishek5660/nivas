Who we are

Built in the valley,
for homes in the valley.

We are a workshop-led studio, not a reseller. Every panel is measured on your site, cut on our machines and installed by people we employ — which is why we can put ten years behind the carcass without flinching.

  • Honest quotations. Line by line, so you can see what every part costs before you commit.
  • Moisture-ready materials. BWR and 710-grade marine ply, because our winters are damp.
  • Our own install team. No sub-contracted labour turning up at your door.
  • Service that continues. A hinge in year three is still our problem, not yours.
